    declare-zsh#

    declare-zsh is a parser for zplugin commands in .zshrc. It allows to perform the following actions on .zshrc from the command-line:

    • enable and disable selected plugins and snippets,
    • add plugins and snippets,
    • delete plugins and snippets.
